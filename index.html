How to play CIPHER
🔢
Drag the 9 numbers into the 3×3 grid
➕
Every row, column, and diagonal must sum to the same number — the cipher sum
🔍
The cipher sum is never shown — you need to figure it out yourself
💡
Tap Check to see which lines are correct. Up to 3 hints available.
🗓️
One puzzle a day — the same for everyone, worldwide. A new cipher drops at midnight.
